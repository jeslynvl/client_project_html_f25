Career Exploration Hub

Finding Your Path at UMSI

Not sure which career path is right for you? The Career Development Office (CDO) provides resources and guidance to help UMSI students explore opportunities in UX design, data science, librarianship, information policy, and more. Career exploration is about discovering where your skills, values, and interests meet real-world opportunities.

AI Prompt Resource Guide: Career Exploration

Learn how to use AI tools effectively for career exploration. This guide provides sample prompts you can adapt to research career paths, prepare for interviews, and discover job opportunities in your field of interest.

Current Students: Log in to UMSI CareerLink

CareerLink is UMSI’s exclusive career portal where students can access job postings, internships, and employer connections. Log in to UMSI CareerLink with your U-M credentials to get started.

Next Steps

Ready to explore further? Meet with a Career Coach to map out your path. They can help you connect your skills and interests with UMSI’s wide range of career opportunities.
