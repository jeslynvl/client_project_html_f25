Networking and Building Connections

Build Your Professional Network and Connections

Developing strong professional connections is a key part of launching your career. The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub offers guidance, practical tips, and examples to help you make meaningful connections with peers, alumni, and industry professionals. Whether you are reaching out for the first time or refining your networking strategy, you’ll find everything you need to grow your network and advance your career.

Networking FAQ

  • How do I start networking if I’m shy? Start small by connecting with classmates, student groups, or alumni via email or LinkedIn.
  • What is an informational interview? A conversation with a professional to learn about their career path and industry insights.
  • How do I follow up after an event? Send a brief thank-you email, mention something you discussed, and keep the connection warm.
